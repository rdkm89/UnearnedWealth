 Well fare the Taverns yet , that though they cosen'd never so much , would downe with it one way or other : and their Iacks , go agen ; now tell your Mris.  & that wil hinder her somwhat .  Nay sweet Cosen , make no uprore for my reputations sake ; here youth there 's two shillings more , commend me to your Mistresse .  And then we came for your warrant , to vind all these men agen .  Vp to a lodging in St. Gileses sir .  My vather told it me upon his death-bed , and charg'd me on his blessing , never to open my mouth to man , woman , nor child , zo I told no body but vokes on 't .
