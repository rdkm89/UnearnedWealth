 Nay prithee sweet Parnell content , & hark thee —  VVe acknowledge it .  Deare Father and Mother pardon us but this time .  Never , and therefore hold your peace .  Oh! —  VVeepe .   Whether now , hoe ?  Ha , ha , ha , How 's this ?  Vanisht , she and the Piper both vanisht , no bodie knowes how .  I , I , Away , away .  Nay hold Parnel hold .  Peace Parnell .  Peace good Parnell .  Fie Parnel fie .  Alas poore Parnel .  Is 't possible ?  Let it be so .
