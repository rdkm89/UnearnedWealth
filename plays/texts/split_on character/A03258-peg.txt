 No comfort !  My Mamilion .  Mamilion , ho Mamilion , Mamilion .  My friend , my Sweet-heart , my Mamilion .  'T is folly to dissemble yie sir , I am one .  Yes Sir .  A matter of sixe yeares Sir .  Yes when I pleas'd .  T is folly to dissemble ; twice a Weeke he never fail'd me .  T is folly to speake worse of him than he is .  He pleas'd me well Sir , like a proper man .  Onely his flesh felt cold .  Gentleman like , but blacke blacke points and all .
