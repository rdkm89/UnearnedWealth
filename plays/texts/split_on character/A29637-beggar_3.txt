 He should have wit and knavery too , Sir :  For he was an Attorney , till he was pitch'd over the Bar . And , from that fall , he was taken up a Knight o' the Post ; and so he continued , till he was degraded at the whipping-post ; and from thence he ran resolutely into this course . His cunning in the Law , and the others labour with the Muses are dedicate to your service ; and for my self , I 'l fight for you .  Will you make us happy in serving you ? have you any Enemies ? shall we fight under you ? will you be our Captain ?
